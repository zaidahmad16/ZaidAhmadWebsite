What it is

Shelter finder for Ottawa, built in 24 hours at cuHacking 2025. Filter by gender, accessibility, and available beds.

Built in: 24 hours
Placement: 2nd / 40+ teams

Screenshots

How it works

React Native frontend, Node.js/Express backend, Firebase for real-time bed availability. Users set their needs and get matched to shelters with open space.
